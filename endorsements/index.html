Who has endorsed Antonio Baciu for Cathedral City Council District 5?

Antonio Baciu’s campaign has earned endorsements from Cathedral City Mayor Raymond Gregory, Cathedral City Councilmembers Nancy Ross and Rita Lamb, Cathedral City Parks and Community Events Commission members Ashley McGullam and Erin Hyland, Riverside County Supervisor V. Manuel Perez, education leaders from PSUSD and College of the Desert, neighboring city leaders, the Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce, and local community organizers.

This endorsement coalition reflects broad support across Cathedral City, District 5, the Coachella Valley, education, business, and community service networks.

Voter Questions

Endorsement FAQs

Clear, search-friendly answers for voters looking up Antonio Baciu endorsements, District 5 support, and how to add their name.

Who has endorsed Antonio Baciu?

Antonio Baciu is endorsed by Cathedral City Mayor Raymond Gregory, Cathedral City Councilmembers Nancy Ross and Rita Lamb, Riverside County Supervisor V. Manuel Perez, PSUSD Board Member Madonna Gerrell, COD Board Trustees Ron Oden, Mary Jane Sanchez, and Dr. Joel Kinnamon, the Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce, and additional regional and community leaders.

Which Cathedral City officials have endorsed Antonio Baciu?

Cathedral City Mayor Raymond Gregory, Cathedral City Councilmembers Nancy Ross and Rita Lamb, and Cathedral City Parks and Community Events Commission members Ashley McGullam and Erin Hyland are listed as endorsers of Antonio Baciu for City Council District 5.

What community and organization endorsements are listed?

The endorsement list includes the Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce and LGBT Community Organizer Raymond Lafleur, along with local and regional civic leaders.

How can someone endorse Antonio Baciu?

Residents, neighbors, community leaders, business owners, and organizations can use the support form to endorse Antonio Baciu and provide the way their name should be listed publicly.
